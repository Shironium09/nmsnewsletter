Comparative Study on Different Types of Computers

Introduction

Computers are machines that can be programmed to perform different kinds of task like arithmetic or logical operations. Computers are a critical part of our modern world as they are backbone of industries that use them for solving various computational problems. Early computers were meant to be used for calculations, being built to automate tedious task. Conventionally, modern computers can now be used for a variety of purposes such as gaming, document editors, and communication.

Discussion

From micro computers for personal task to supercomputer for handling complex simulations, these computers all have specific features and functionalities that differentiates them from each other. Computers can be categorized and defined by its build, computational power, energy efficiency, and area of application.
